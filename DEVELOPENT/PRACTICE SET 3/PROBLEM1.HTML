            Zomato was founded as FoodieBay in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah who worked for Bain & Company. The
        website started as a restaurant-listing-and-recommendation portal. They renamed the company Zomato in 2010 as
        they were unsure if they would "just stick to food" and also to avoid a potential naming conflict with
        eBay.[8][9][10]
